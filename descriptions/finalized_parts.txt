Electret Microphone Amplifier - MAX9814
- http://AfmBOorloo779mgIO4CJQvcvtxGO6InRc07cuzwLM6ZgvbfrqGNLS_gw
- Comes with a microphone, up to 20kHz (limit of human hearing range)
- the default 'max gain' is 60dB, but can be set to 40dB or 50dB by jumpering the Gain pin to VCC or ground
- Pins: AR, out, Gain, Vdd, GND
- Output is 2Vpp max with a 1.25V DC offset
- Works well w/ ADC input of 3.3V (good for STM32)

I2S MEMS Microphone Breakout - SPH0645LM4H
- https://www.adafruit.com/product/3421?srsltid=AfmBOoqp2WRJInd3YiHxNqGG1jnJGZDeQnyUoE2Xf0Osnp9s8FyEq0c0
- I2S → high speed, meant for sound
- No analog conversion required! Instead of an analog output, there are three digital pins: Clock, Data, and Left-Right (Word Select) Clock. When connected to your microcontroller/computer, the 'I2S Controller' will drive the clock and word-select pins at a high frequency and read out the data from the microphone. 

Stereo 3.7W Class D Audio Amplifier - MAX98306
